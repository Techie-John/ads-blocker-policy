Privacy Policy for ICP Checker

Last updated: May 9, 2026

Overview ICP Checker is a Chrome extension that helps you evaluate LinkedIn profiles against your Ideal Customer Profile (ICP). We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data the extension touches and what we do with it.

Data We Collect We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our own servers. We have no servers.

How the Extension Works When you click "Analyse Profile" on a LinkedIn profile page, the extension:

Takes a screenshot of the visible tab
Reads the visible text content of the page
Sends this data directly to the Groq API (api.groq.com) to generate an ICP analysis
This data is sent solely to process your request and is subject to Groq's Privacy Policy.

Your API Key Your Groq API key is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in chrome.storage.local. It is never transmitted to anyone other than Groq when making API requests.

Your ICP Definition Your ICP text is stored locally on your device using chrome.storage.local. It is never transmitted to anyone other than Groq as part of the analysis prompt.

Data Sharing We do not sell, share, or transfer any data to third parties. The only third party involved is Groq, which processes the screenshot and page text to return an analysis result.

LinkedIn Data Profile data (screenshots and page text) is used only in real-time to generate an analysis. It is not stored, logged, or retained after the API call completes.

Changes to This Policy We may update this policy occasionally. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
